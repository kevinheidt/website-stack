## StoryBrand Framework

**The Hero:** Homeowner wanting to upgrade their home  
**The Problem:** Outdated electrical that can't handle modern life — old panels, unsafe wiring, no capacity for EVs, kitchen renos, or home additions  
**The Guide:** ABC Electrical — trusted experts who make it easy  
**The Plan:** 1) Free Assessment → 2) Clear Quote → 3) Professional Install  
**The Success:** A beautiful, safe, modern home that works for their lifestyle

## NEW DESIGN SYSTEM

### Typography (Google Fonts)
- **Headlines:** **Playfair Display** — elegant, editorial, premium feel
- **Body:** **Inter** — clean, modern, highly readable  
- **Accent/Labels:** **JetBrains Mono** — technical, trustworthy

### Color Palette
| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary (Dark) | Deep Navy | `#0a1628` |
| Secondary | Warm Copper | `#c87941` |
| Accent | Soft Cream | `#f5f0e8` |
| Text | Charcoal | `#1a1a1a` |
| Light | Off-White | `#faf9f7` |

**Why:** Navy + copper feels premium, trustworthy, warm. Better for renovations than corporate blue.
