# 🍞 Kettle & Grain Bakery - Design Brief

## Brand Identity
**Name:** Kettle & Grain Bakery  
**Tagline:** Slow bread. Real grain. Okanagan made.  
**Vibe:** Honest craft. Raw wood, linen, natural light. Okanagan farm meets European bakery. NOT hipster-precious.

## Color Palette
- **Warm cream:** `#f5f0e8` (background)
- **Deep charcoal:** `#1c1c1c` (text, accents)
- **Wheat gold:** `#c8922a` (accent, buttons)
- **Dusty sage:** `#7a8c6e` (secondary accent)

## Typography
- **Headings:** Playfair Display (serif, elegant)
- **Body:** Inter (sans-serif, clean, readable)
- **System fallback:** Georgia, serif / -apple-system, sans-serif

## Design Requirements
1. **Fully custom** — no templates, no themes
2. **Mobile responsive** — mobile-first approach
3. **Hero section** with full-width bread image and overlaid tagline
4. **Smooth scroll navigation**
5. **Subtle grain/texture CSS background** on some sections
6. **Open kitchen "watch us bake"** section with large image
7. **Farm partner cards** with photos
8. **Footer** with hours, address, Instagram handle (@kettleandgrain)

## Image Sources (Unsplash)
Use these direct URLs or search terms:
1. **"sourdough bread artisan"** - Hero image
2. **"wheat field golden okanagan"** - Farm partners section
3. **"bakery open kitchen"** - Open kitchen section
4. **"heritage grain flour mill"** - Our Story page
5. **"farmer wheat okanagan"** - Farm partner cards

## Pages Structure
1. **Home** — hero, featured breads, our story teaser, farm partners, hours/location
2. **Our Bread** — product grid with descriptions (sourdoughs, pastries, seasonal)
3. **Our Story** — Marcus's background, grain sourcing, farm partnerships, open kitchen
4. **Order / Visit** — pickup pre-order form, hours, address, Google Maps embed

## Technical Requirements
- **Build from scratch** in HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS (or Astro if preferred)
- **No Astra theme** or any page builder
- **Save to:** `/Users/brightsidehome/.openclaw/workspace/website-stack/kettle-grain/`
- **All pages functional** and visually polished
- **Form static** (no backend needed, just HTML structure)

## Visual Elements
- **Grain/texture backgrounds** using CSS patterns
- **Card layouts** for farm partners and bread items
- **Full-width imagery** with text overlays
- **Subtle animations** on hover (bread cards, buttons)
- **Responsive grid** for product listings

## Navigation
- **Sticky header** with logo and navigation
- **Mobile hamburger menu**
- **Smooth scroll** to sections
- **Active state** highlighting
